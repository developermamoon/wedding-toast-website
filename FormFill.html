What type of speech best matches your tone?
Funny

A humorous tone can be a great way to break the ice and get the guests laughing. A best man can use jokes, puns, or funny anecdotes to entertain the audience and add some levity to the event.

Touching

A sentimental tone can be a good choice for a best man who wants to express his emotions and offer heartfelt congratulations to the happy couple.

Inspirational

A inspirational tone can be a good choice for a best man who wants to offer words of wisdom and encouragement to the couple.

Wild

A tone that is witty or sarcastic, to add some fun or irreverence to the speech. This approach is useful if the giver is close to the couple, and the wedding atmosphere is casual and relaxed.
